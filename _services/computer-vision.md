We implement Computer Vision solutions at the edge or at the core, with the necessary infrastructure for annotations, training, and MLOps.

Infrastructure can be developed in-house, or use AWS, GCP, Azure or other cloud distributions.

We develop the dataset infrastructure, annotations framework, and auto-annotations, as needed. We also develop the models and MLOps infrastructure to run training on the dataset, and inference on the data sources.
