We design and implement:
* AI for Digital Health
* AI workflows for administrative and revenue cycle tasks
* RPA and IPA automation
* Business rules engines

Current Projects:
* Pre-op screening process: Architecture and proof of concept for parsing allergies, medical conditions, medications from incoming fax queue. Implementation uses OCR to parse fax images. Patient and provider data is deidentified, and large language models are used to extract the required medical information in JSON format.
* Python harness for Electronic Medical Records application: Architecture and implementation of python module to control electronic medical records application programmatically.
* Revenue Cycle Management: Architecture and system design for fully automated processing of health insurance claims for Durable Medical Equipment.
