We architect and implement data lakes and pipelines tailored to Enterprise customer needs, on-premise, on-VPC, and at scale.

The tools of choice for data engineering may be AWS, GCP or Azure. We also use Snowflake and Databricks, and develop the ingestion framework, data cleaning, and integration with Business Intelligence front-end dashboarding tools, or with AI infrastructure that uses the ingested data as a source.
